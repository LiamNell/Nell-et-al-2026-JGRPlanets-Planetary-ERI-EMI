This repository archives the primary field datasets supporting the study "Surface-Based Geophysical Monitoring of Conductive Planetary Environments: An Integrated ERI-EMI Approach."

Core Datasets:
•	 Electrical Resistivity Imaging (ERI): organized by season and formatted as standard Iris Instruments Syscal .csv files.
•	 Electromagnetic Induction (EMI): organized by season and provided as .dat files generated by the GF Instruments' CMD MiniExplorer 6L system.
•	 Time-Domain Reflectometry (TDR): Continuous soil moisture records spanning June 2024 to February 2026.
•	 Groundwater Data: Continuous hydrostatic pressure and temperature logger records spanning December 2024 to February 2026.
•	 Note: Both continuous datasets are stored within February 2026/ directory.
